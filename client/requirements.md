## Packages
framer-motion | Essential for the cinematic scroll animations, 3D transforms, and background effects requested in the design brief.

## Notes
- Enforcing a strict dark mode theme to match the cinematic fintech aesthetic.
- Global trade and logistics visualizations are built purely with SVG and Framer Motion to avoid heavy external 3D libraries while maintaining a premium feel.
- Animations use staggered intersection observers (via framer-motion) to trigger as the user scrolls.
